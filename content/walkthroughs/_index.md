Welcome to the `Unitools Universe` user guide! This guide shows you how to get started using the suite of tools centered on `Uniswap`, an automated market maker service built on Ethereum. We will show you how sign up for a centralized exchange account, how to move Ethereum from that account to a trusted wallet, and how to swap that Ethereum for other tokens on Uniswap.

There are also many tools being built to help analyze Uniswap, protect you from scams, and in general gain a better user experience. We will focus on two here, `Unicrypt` with its first product the Proof of Liquidity app, and `DexTools`, a service that allows you to better analyze Uniswap transactions.
